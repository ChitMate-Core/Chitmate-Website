Frequently asked questions

Everything to know before launch.

Does ChitMate process payments?

No. ChitMate is designed to organize groups, track contributions and balances, and provide reminders. Payments are handled outside the app by the group.

Who is ChitMate for?

It is built for trusted circles such as friends, family members, neighbors, colleagues, and community groups working toward shared financial goals.

When will the app launch?

The public release is planned for the coming months. Join the waitlist to receive beta invitations and launch updates.

Will ChitMate be available on iPhone and Android?

The launch plan includes both iOS and Android. Platform availability may be phased during beta testing.
